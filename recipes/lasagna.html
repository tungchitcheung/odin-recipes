Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce, and parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes at 375 degrees F. Uncover lasagna after 25 minutes.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving for 15 minutes.
